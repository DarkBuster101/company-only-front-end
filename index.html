DONGJIN SEMICHEM, Mother Company in South Korea.

After being founded in 1967, DONGJIN SEMICHEM has grown steadily along with the development of South Korea's electronic materials market. Based on years of experience and competitive expertise in advanced materials technology, DONGJIN SEMICHEM now holds a place among the world's leading chemical corporations. (22 Factories, 8 Offices, 1 R&D center all the world)
